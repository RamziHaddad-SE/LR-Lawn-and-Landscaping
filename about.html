Our Story

Local roots in Ramer, Alabama

LR Lawn & Landscaping started the way a lot of good things in the country do — with a truck, some equipment, and a reputation for doing what we said we'd do. We're a local family business, and that's not a tagline. Our name is on every job, and the people you talk to are the same people who show up to do the work.

We built this business around the jobs other folks pass on: the overgrown lots, the back fields nobody's touched in a season, the standing-weekly yards that need to look right every single time. Whether it's a quarter-acre in town or acreage out in the county, we bring the right tools and the same effort.

Most of all, we want to earn your business — not just win it once. That means fair prices, honest answers, and work we're proud to put our name behind.
